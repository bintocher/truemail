Your mail stays on your computer.

truemail is a desktop email client for managing multiple mailboxes in one place. It connects directly to your provider and keeps synchronized mail data locally on your device.

Desktop application · in active development

Full email client

Read, compose, send, search, organize and delete messages, attachments and folders.

Direct connection

OAuth, IMAP and SMTP connections go directly from the desktop app to the selected mail provider.

Local-first privacy

Mailbox content is not uploaded to a truemail cloud service. Credentials are kept in the operating system keychain.

How truemail uses Google data

When a user connects Gmail, truemail requests access needed to operate as an email client: synchronizing folders and messages, displaying attachments, composing and sending mail, and applying user-requested mailbox changes. Google user data is processed locally by the desktop application and is not used for advertising, profiling, or training AI models.
